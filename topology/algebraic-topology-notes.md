## 1. Fundamental Concepts

We begin with the concept of **Simplicial Complexes**. A sequence of Abelian groups $C_n$ connected by boundary operators $\partial_n$:
$$\dots \xrightarrow{\partial_{n+1}} C_n \xrightarrow{\partial_n} C_{n-1} \xrightarrow{\partial_{n-1}} \dots$$
satisfying the condition $\partial_n \circ \partial_{n+1} = 0$.
